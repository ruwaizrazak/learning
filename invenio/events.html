Bridge Design

Showcase Your Engineering Skills

Rules
  • No of participants in a team can be 2-3.
  • Designing of bridge should be done by the provided objects. We will popsicle sticks,feviquick as adhesive.
  • Use of other materials will lead to disqualifications.
  • Sticks can be altered physically by cutting , bending, notching etc. The number of sticks are limited to 250.
  • Length of bridge should not be more than 50 cm and width 15 cm.
  • The model built by each team will be tested till failure (yielding) by applying an incremental static load.
  • In case of any discrepancies, the decision taken by the judges and the council will be the final verdict.
Testing the Bridge
  • We will place your bridge on the end of two tables.The load will be applied through a hook that connects a steel platform that will be laid upon the span of the bridge.
  • The bridge model will be loaded till failure.
  • The bridge will be scored on how well the material has been used to support the load. The efficiency will be calculated as the ratio of ultimate load capacity and the bridge weight.
  • The procedure will be same for every team.

FIFA

The name defines it.

Rules
  • All games will be classic 1 vs 1 in multiplayer mode.
  • There will be no practising allowed, participants are only allowed to change necessary controls and check them.
  • Keyboards will be provided. Participants can use their own joysticks if necessary.
  • In case of a draw, match will be restarted with golden goal rule (same team must be selected).
  • In case of unintentional disconnection, admin will decide on how to proceed.
  • If game is within half time and no goals have been scored, the game will be restarted with same teams for a full time game. If not, the game will be played for the remaining time based on what time it disconnected with scores being carried over.
  • If at any time, a player intentionally disconnects /exits the game, he/she will be disqualified.in case of alt-tabbing, the admin will decide whether it was intentional /mistake and what action must be taken with disqualification being maximum penalty.
  • Teams can be either clubs or countries according to user's will (classic XI and world XI not allowed).
  • The prelims and league will be classic 4 min half time matches. Quarter and semis will all be 5 min half time matches and finals will be 6 min half time matches.
  • The 20 players having qualified will be drawn randomly into 4 groups from which 2 each will qualify to last 8 (quarter finals).
  • The admin's decision will be final.
